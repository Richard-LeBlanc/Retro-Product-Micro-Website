The Deely-Bopper name:

Created by Stephen Askin the Deely-Bopper was a quick success. armed with just a dream and a limited supply of prototype Deely-Boppers, Stephen headed off to the 1981 Los Angeles Street Fair. With his unique brand and fun loving attitude, it was a resounding success. He sold over 800 that day, and created an instant sensation. Deely-Boppers are one of the most popular novelty items of the last century. and is it any surprise?

With a powerful brand and unique passion for his product, the Deely-Bopper name and his fan-base grew in size. From coast to coast the name people trust with fashionable and quirky headgear is Deely-Bopper. Durable and sensation, ever since that first day at the Street Fair, people put their faith in us for a reason.

Stephen Askin:

Hi, Stephen here, I'm honoured to present myself to you all. I have been a marketer for years bringing products people love to the people that love them. I never imagined I would be able to bring a product of my own creation to a group of so many. I feel blessed to have so many supporters already, and as such plan to continue to bring the highest quality Deely-Boppers at a low low price for all to enjoy!
